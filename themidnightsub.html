The Midnight Sub
Right place to fulfil your midnight cravings
Background
The Midnight Sub is a new cloud kitchen restaurant, aiming to start its business in Mumbai, India. The restaurant needs a website where customers can browse through the offerings, customise their meals, and place orders.
Process
Project Brief
An entrepreneur wants to open a new cloud kitchen restaurant that delivers food to nearby surroundings. The highlight of the website should be the customisation and ordering system. Assuming the entrepreneur is your client, plan and design the website.
Location and neighbourhood
Business of a cloud kitchen restaurant primarily depends on deliveries, and hence the neighbourhood that the restaurant caters to becomes essentially important. I researched a bit about cloud kitchens and summarised my findings in broad points.

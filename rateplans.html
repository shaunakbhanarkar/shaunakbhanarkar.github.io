Overview
___
OYO is a leading hotel booking app in India and across the world. This project introduces multiple ways to book rooms in a hotel based on user's preferences.
Problem
___
OYO does not provide flexible options to users in terms of cancellation policy and payment. Additionally, the attach rates of meals on the OYO app are significantly low.
Solution
___
Introduce the options in the form of rate plans along with room type in the booking journey.
Why rate plans
___
All the platforms in this domain offer their booking options in the form of rate plans and the users are accustomed to this flow.
The constraint
___
OYO is known for its unique 3-step booking journey. This essentially means that a user can book a hotel on the OYO in just 3 clicks. To adhere with this, introducing any mandatory additional step in the booking journey was not allowed.

___

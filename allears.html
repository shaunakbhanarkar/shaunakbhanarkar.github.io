Background
___
In India, mental health is a domain that remains largely ignored by people due to a variety of reasons. All Ears is an early stage startup on a mission to promote mental wellbeing. We are trying to understand the Indian context and explore the possibilities of catering to mental health issues using technology.
Research and Insights
___
Initial research about the mental health scenario in India yielded the following results.
Currently, two polar options exist to help open up and talk about feelings. First, theraphy sessions, which are expensive and still have an associated social stigma. Second, online peer-to-peer chat platforms, which are unmoderated and are often sources of random unsolicited comments.
  • Mental health is not thought as a problem to be solved
  • Most people do not understand how therapy or even listening can help
  • The number of people receiving mental health consultation is far less than the number of experts available.
  • There are a lot of psychology students and graduates willing to become empathetic listeners to get practical experience
Goal
___
To make mental health support and services inclusive and accessible using technology

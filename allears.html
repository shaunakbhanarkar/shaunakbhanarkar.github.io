Overview
___
All Ears is a mental health app that connects people with mental health experts. I got an opportunity to work with this early stage startup to create the design system and design the user interface of the app.
Research and insights
What are the problems in Indian context?
___
Currently, for someone to open up and talk about their feelings, there are two polar options.
1
Therapy: Therapy sessions are expensive and still have an associated social stigma.
2
Online platforms: Online peer-to-peer chat platforms are unmoderated and are often sources of random unsolicited comments.
Goal
___
To make mental health support and services easily accessible using technology
Version 1
All Ears app
___
Salient features:
1
People who need mental health support can anonymously talk about their feelings and issues.
2
Empaths are psychology experts willing to listen and provide mental health support.
3
Holding a conversation and consistent exchange of thoughts is essential, and hence the chats are temporary, and they are deleted once the conversation is over.
4
Social profile of empaths helps in maintaining trust, with badges about their listening skills.
5
One can become an empath by taking up specialized listening courses and getting certified.
Testing and further scope
___
The beta version of the app was tested with 60 participants from different psychology colleges in India. We received positive feedback, and many psychology graduates are willing to become empathetic listeners on the platform.

The team is now working to define the product rollout and market strategy to make the app public.
Reflection
What did I learn?
___
1
Real world challenges: There is a huge difference between designing a concept and designing a practical, real world application that will be used by people. Thinking about all kind of use cases and user flows during the design process is critical.
2
Collaboration with other stakeholders: Working closely with co-founders, developers, and graphic designers has taught me the importance of communication and presentation skills.

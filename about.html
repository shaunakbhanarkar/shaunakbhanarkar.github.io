My journey
Fall 2018
Where it started 💭
During my pre-final year at college, I was introduced briefly to the concepts of Human Computer Interaction in one of the elective courses. It naturally caught my interest and I found myself searching the Internet about everything related to HCI.
Early 2019
Exploring 🤝
I connected with many people in the field of HCI and design, and came to know what exactly this is about. People with a variety of expertise, such as User Research, UX Design, Product Design, UX Writing etc. helped me take the right steps.
Fall 2019
The learning begins 📚
Based on the recommendations of experienced and skilled professionals in the UX community, I started the learning curve by taking online courses on Coursera.
Mid 2020
One step at a time 💪
By this time, I had successfully completed two UX based specialisations from Coursera - Interaction Design Specialisation by UC San Diego, and UI/UX Design Specialisation by Cal Arts. I took the extra step and did the Graphic Design Specialisation by Cal Arts to improve my visual design skills.
Late 2020 & Early 2021
Getting ready 👨‍💻
After learning the basics, I decided to take up some passion projects on my own to practice and apply what I had learnt in the past months. Out of interest, I also started writing articles on Medium. Finally, I consolidated my work in the form of this portfolio and updated my Dribbble profile.
What next?
I'm excited to start my career in design where I can drive my passion to create amazing products, and learn and grow further! 🤩
